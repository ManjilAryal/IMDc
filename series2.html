Taaza Khabar (Hindi: ताज़ा ख़बर, romanized: Tāzā K͟habar) is an Indian comedy thriller television series on Disney+ Hotstar. It was written by Abbas Dalal and Hussain Dalal and directed by Himank Gaur. It stars Bhuvan Bam, Shriya Pilgaonkar, J. D. Chakravarthy, Deven Bhojani, Prathamesh Parab, Nitya Mathur, and Shilpa Shukla.[citation needed] In April 2024, Disney+ Hotstar confirmed that the series had been renewed for a second season.

The web series is OTT debut of Youtuber Bhuvan Bam.
